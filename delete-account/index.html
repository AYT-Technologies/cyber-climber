Delete Account & Data

Cyber Climber does not require an account. All data is stored locally on your device.

What data does Cyber Climber store?

Cyber Climber stores the following data locally on your device only:

  • Your high score
  • Sound and vibration settings

No data is stored on any external server.

How to delete your data on Android

  1. Open Settings on your device
  2. Go to Apps → Cyber Climber
  3. Tap Storage → Clear Data
  4. All local game data will be removed

Alternatively, uninstalling the app will remove all stored data.

How to delete your data on iOS

  1. Delete the Cyber Climber app from your device
  2. All local game data will be automatically removed

Advertising Data

Cyber Climber uses Google AdMob for advertising. To manage or delete data collected by Google, visit:

Google Data & Privacy Settings
