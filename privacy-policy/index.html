Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 8, 2026

AYT Technologies ("we", "our", or "us") operates the Cyber Climber mobile application. This page informs you of our policies regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of personal information when you use our app.

Information We Collect

Cyber Climber does not directly collect any personal information. The app stores your high score locally on your device only.

Third-Party Services

Our app uses the following third-party services that may collect information:

  • Google AdMob - We use AdMob to display advertisements. AdMob may collect device identifiers, IP addresses, and usage data to serve personalized or non-personalized ads. For more information, see Google's Privacy Policy.

Data Storage

All game data (high score, settings) is stored locally on your device. We do not transmit or store any personal data on external servers.

Children's Privacy

Cyber Climber is suitable for all ages. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us.

Advertising

We display the following types of ads:

  • Banner ads - Displayed at the bottom of the game screen
  • Rewarded ads - Optional video ads that allow you to continue after game over

You can opt out of personalized advertising through your device settings.

Permissions

Cyber Climber requires the following permissions:

  • Internet - Required to load and display advertisements
  • Network State - Required to check connectivity for ad loading
  • Wi-Fi State - Required for ad network connectivity

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date.
