Frequently Asked Questions

  • How do I continue after game over? — Watch a rewarded ad to continue from where you left off.
  • How is my score calculated? — You earn one point for each platform you land on.
  • How do I turn off sound or vibration? — Tap the settings icon in the top right corner of the game screen.
  • My high score was lost. — High scores are stored locally on your device. Reinstalling the app will reset your high score.
