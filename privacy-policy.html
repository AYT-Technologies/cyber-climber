This Privacy Policy describes how AYT Technologies ("we", "us", or "our") handles information in the Cyber Climber mobile game ("the App").

Information We Collect

We do not directly collect any personal information. The App uses Google AdMob to display advertisements, which may collect certain data as described below.

Advertising (Google AdMob)

The App uses Google AdMob to show banner and rewarded video advertisements. AdMob may collect device identifiers and usage data to serve relevant ads. For more information, please review Google's Privacy Policy.

Data Storage

The App stores your high score locally on your device using standard device storage. This data is not transmitted to our servers.

Children's Privacy

The App is not directed to children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.
